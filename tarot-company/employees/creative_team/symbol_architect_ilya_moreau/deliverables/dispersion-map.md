## Spread Shape Definition

As agreed in cross-session (I-1):

**Coherent spread:** All three selected cards have at least two axes within ±0.25 of each other on those axes. The spread points at one region of polarity space from different angles.

**Tensioned spread:** At least one card is more than 0.60 apart from another card on a single axis. The spread contains genuine polarity conflict.

Stage A outputs `spread_shape: "coherent"` or `spread_shape: "tensioned"` alongside the card selections. Amara's register rules use this signal.

## Notes for Voice Spec

*Flagged for Amara:*

- **Image anchors** are placeholders pending illustrator engagement. Each anchor describes a visual element that should appear in the final card art. Until art exists, these are descriptive references for the voice to draw on.
- **Antipodes** feed the two-interpretation structure (shadow/gift). The voice receives the antipode card_id alongside the selected card_id.
- **Arcana type** should modulate register: Major → less drift, more weight, more silence at close. Minor → more drift latitude.
- **The Mirror** and **The Veil** have strong Obscurity signals. The voice should not attempt to resolve this — both cards require the interpreter to sit with what cannot be seen.
