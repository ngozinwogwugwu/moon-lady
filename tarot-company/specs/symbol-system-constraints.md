## Summary

- Six polarity axes define the symbolic geometry
- Stage A extracts a structured feature vector from transcripts
- Arcana Gate controls Major Arcana eligibility (intensity + scope thresholds)
- Selection uses weighted cosine similarity + diversity enforcement
- 18-card MVP ontology: 6 Major (extreme vectors), 12 Minor (mid-range)
- Cards assigned to Past / Present / Future positions
- No literal keyword matching permitted
