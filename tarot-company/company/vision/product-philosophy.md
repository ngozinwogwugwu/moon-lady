# Product Philosophy

How we make decisions. Vision explains why we exist. This explains how the system must behave.

---

**1. Symbolism as Compression** — Distill speech into archetypal form. Never keyword-match. Create interpretive space, not answers.

**2. Drift Is a Feature** — Nudge, suggest, linger. Never diagnose or resolve. Preserve curiosity, projection, meaning-making. Drift must remain bounded.

**3. No Deterministic Language** — Avoid "will happen," "destined," "guarantees." Use "may invite," "you may notice," "points toward." We frame, not predict.

**4. Major Arcana Rarity** — Identity shifts, structural transitions, decision points. Must remain rare and feel earned.

**5. Minor Arcana Normalcy** — Daily posture, small tensions, ordinary adjustments. Most days are minor days.

**6. Ethical Boundary** — No therapy replacement, medical/legal/financial advice, supernatural claims, or healing promises. Perspective, not salvation.

**7. Ritual Without Dogma** — Daily cadence, scarcity (one reading/day), expanded readings on special intervals. Scarcity protects meaning.

**8. User Agency** — Invites reflection only. Advice reserved for lunar/seasonal events and aggregated readings.

**9. Personalization** — Aesthetic → archetypal → adaptive. Must enhance reflection, not reduce symbolic ambiguity.

**10. What Breaks It** — Unlimited readings, frequent Major Arcana, literal interpretations, predictive claims, aggressive monetization, gamification. The product must remain: Quiet. Intentional. Contained.

**11. Session Success** — User becomes more attentive, connects symbol to lived experience, feels curiosity not certainty, wants to return.

This philosophy governs all future features. Violations must be reconsidered.

---

## Appendix: Symbol Expansion Principle

Symbols may expand but must remain geometrically representable in polarity space, may not collapse into literal keyword mapping, Major Arcana must remain extreme vectors, Minor Arcana must represent mid-range patterns. Interpretation must preserve drift.
